# Source Document: Productio Platform (2019-2020)
**Context:** Unified engagement platform for consultants and coaches
**Relevance to SAX:** The accountability layer design

## Core Thesis
Productio was a unified engagement platform designed to help coaches and consultants track whether their clients were executing on their goals. It functioned as a "backcasting tool that drives accountability across sales, service, and success by measuring the realization and utilization of human capital assets."

## Key Mechanisms

### Delivery Channels
1. **Mobile**
2. **Web**
3. **Conversational**

### Core Workflows
1. **Strategy Workflow:** Defining the overarching goals.
2. **Goals Workflow:** Setting specific, measurable targets.
3. **Task Workflow:** Managing day-to-day actions.
4. **Accountability Workflow:** Tracking progress and ensuring follow-through.

### Functional Modules

## Application to The Exchange Protocol
Productio provides the **missing operational layer** in the SAX governance architecture. It is the platform prototype for how the Exchange Protocol runs at scale.

The Productio workflows map directly to the SAX model:
* **Strategy Workflow** = The athlete's personal business model discovery plan.
* **Goals Workflow** = The specific learning and performance targets for each engagement.
* **Task Workflow** = The day-to-day project work with the business partner.
* **Accountability Workflow** = The TAC-verified progress reporting back to the athletic department.

Productio demonstrates how the Exchange tracks whether the protocol is working. The decision of whether to rebuild this capability, partner with an existing tool, or use Productio as a design specification must be documented in the **Chapter Requirements (ARC-001-REQ)**.
