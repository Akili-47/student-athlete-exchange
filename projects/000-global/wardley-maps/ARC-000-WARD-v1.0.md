## Evolution Positioning — Narrative

### Genesis layer (The Exchange occupies this alone)

**ECHO Substrate**
The execution intelligence substrate sitting beneath
every Exchange session. Judgment graph, dual-hash
attribution, living dataset. No competitor has built
this. Cannot be replicated without 4–7 years of
equivalent deployment. This is the moat.

**Assumption Delta**
The gap between where research places a component
and where a domain expert places it based on
operational reality. Ungoogleable. Uncapturable by AI.
Permanently valuable. No existing program captures this.

**Judgment Graph**
The living dataset that compounds with every session.
Improves through personnel rotation rather than
degrading. The longer it runs, the harder it is
to replicate.

**ETU Capture**
Structured execution trace at session close.
SCQA format. Co-signed by Map Lead and TAC.
No existing athlete development program does this.

### Custom layer (The Exchange builds here)

**TAC Evaluation**
Domain expert challenge against operational reality.
Custom because TAC qualification criteria are novel —
no existing program requires practitioners who have
operated in the domain within the last five years.

**Domain Strategic Analysis**
Four-phase session format producing a Wardley Map
of a partner organization's competitive landscape.
Custom because the athlete-produced deliverable
validated by domain expert is novel.

**Verified Portfolio**
Dual-hashed, timestamped, athlete-owned portfolio entry.
Moving toward product as the Work Ledger matures.

**TAC Network**
Pipeline of domain experts across sports business,
NIL, military, healthcare, and manufacturing.
Custom at MVP. Product at scale.

### Product layer (commodity tools, used not built)

**Wardley Mapping Session** — Miro boards,
online wardley maps tool, structured four-phase format.

**Work Ledger** — already built, dual-hash design,
Bitcoin-anchored timestamps.

**University Contract** — standard professional
services agreement. Commodity template.

### Commodity layer (use as-is, do not build)

**GitHub** — version control, public repository,
Pages hosting. Free. Permanent. Verifiable.

**Miro** — group session tool. Product moving
toward commodity.

**Resume** — the artifact The Exchange replaces
or supplements. Already fully commoditized.

## Gameplay Visible From This Map

**Land grab:** First mover in genesis space.
TAC network + judgment graph + ECHO substrate
establish position before competitors recognize the space.

**Tower and moat:** Verified portfolio standard (tower)
+ ECHO dataset (moat). The standard is visible and
copyable in theory. The dataset requires years to replicate.

**Open approaches:** Wardley Mapping is CC licensed.
The session format is MIT licensed. Drop the barrier
to adoption at the visible layer. Protect the substrate.

**Standards game:** ETU schema + SCQA format +
dual-hash attribution become the industry standard
for athlete portfolio verification. Once established,
competitors must adopt or remain outside the standard.

**Two-factor:** Athletes produce. Organizations consume.
Each side makes the other more valuable.
Career services cannot replicate this without
the TAC network. The TAC network has no value
without the athlete cohort.
